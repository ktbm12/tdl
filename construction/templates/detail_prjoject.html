The Meridian Plaza

A testament to vertical engineering and sustainable architectural design.

Overview

The Meridian Plaza stands as a pinnacle of modern civil engineering, integrating advanced seismic-resistant technology with a sleek, minimalist aesthetic. Located in the heart of the business district, this 42-story marvel features over 800,000 square feet of premium office space, a triple-height atrium, and state-of-the-art sustainable systems.

TDL Construction oversaw every phase from site excavation through to the final structural glazing, ensuring the highest standards of safety and material integrity were met. The project serves as a new anchor for urban development, blending commercial functionality with communal open spaces.
